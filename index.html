Frequently Asked Questions

1. How much does a standard porta potty rental cost?

Average portable toilet rental prices range from $80–$125/week. Final cost depends on location, duration, and service frequency—especially in high-demand states like Texas or Florida.

2. Do you offer same-day delivery for emergencies?

Yes. Emergency temporary toilet rental is available with 4–8 hour response in most metro areas in #State.

3. Are your ADA units available for home use?

Yes. ADA-compliant portable restroom rental is available for private events, medical needs, or accessibility requirements nationwide.

Standard service is once per week. High-use events may require twice-weekly cleanings to maintain freshwater levels and waste capacity.

No. All portable toilets with handwashing stations are self-contained with onboard freshwater tanks—no external plumbing needed
